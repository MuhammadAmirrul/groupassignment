About Us

Background


Backstreet Arts Gang was established in June 2021 and it was located at Sungai Petani, Kedah. We choose our the name Backstreet Arts Gang because we want a name that is unique and fun for everyone to remember. Our company contains four active partners that manage the company, Mr. Amirrul, Ms. Nazihah, Ms. Nazifa and Ms. Firzana.


Click here to know more about us.

Vision & Mission


"To attract the attention of shoes and arts lover to come to our shop to buy and enjoy wearing our shoes"


Our shop produces high quality and colourful designed canvas shoes that caters to our customers preferences.

Objectives


  • To improve and produce more new arts, design and painting for shoes.
  • To achieve a return on investment of at least 20 percent after-tax in the next fiscal year.
  • To maximize sales by 10% within three years.
  • To ensure our social media awareness stays high by tracking metrics such as website visits.
  • To increase customer satisfaction by reducing the number and frequency of returns and complaints.
